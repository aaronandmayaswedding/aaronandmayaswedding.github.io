Our Story

Maya & Aaron’s love story begins where most love stories begin...a course in symbolic logic. Maya had mistakenly taken the Philosophy 205 class after a counselor told her it was required for her major (it wasn’t). Aaron was smitten with Maya from the first day he saw her in class, and spent a month trying to look cool in front of her (he didn’t). Then on one fateful day, after walking Maya to the bus stop after class, Aaron mustered up the courage to offer her his number, under the pretense of helping her study for the class. 6 years later, Aaron has not answered a single one of Maya’s symbolic logic questions, but she doesn’t seem to mind. Because once you’ve found the love of your life, who needs logic after all?
